SPORTS DAY

The Countdown to Glory Begins! Excitement fills the air as the much-anticipated Sports Day approaches in our school. The annual event, eagerly awaited by students, teachers, and parents alike, promises a day filled with exhilarating sportsmanship, teamwork, and friendly competition. Preparations for the Sports Day are in full swing, with students actively participating in practice sessions, honing their skills in various sports disciplines. The atmosphere is buzzing with energy as athletes train tirelessly, pushing their limits, and striving for excellence. Coaches and teachers work hand in hand, guiding and encouraging the students, nurturing their talents, and instilling in them the values of discipline, perseverance, and fair play.


FOUNDERS DAY

"The Founder of this institution rose from a humble beginning to shine like a luminous star. He succeeded in his mission through his relentless hard work. Sri. V.J.K. Dass was born on 20th November 1921. He did his M.A. in Mysore University. . He got the idea to start up his own tutorial and succeeded in establishing it with a good strength, which continued for 10 years. He started a small primary school at Alwarpet and many celebrities studied in this school. The school was then shifted adjacent to Stella Maris College and had a strength of 3000 children from LKG to 12th Std. This is the first CBSE school in Chennai. The school was later shifted to Adyar. Today the school bears testimony to what an individual can achieve and contribute to the society as his legacy. In order to give him a tribute for his hardwork.Founders day is celebrited every single year."
